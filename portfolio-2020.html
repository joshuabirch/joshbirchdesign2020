Portfolio 2020

– Branding, Web Design, Product Design

Considering I'm coming towards the end of my studies, I thought that my portfolio, and more importantly my brand, needed a fresh overhaul ready for industry life. So, I took a look at some projects on Behance, mapped out what I wanted my brand to represent and got designing!

I wanted to incorporate my favourite color while also providing a clear, aesthetic design that can be transcended across products. As with most portfolio sites, I wanted a grid system on the home page which allows the user to easily access any of my projects instead of them reading all about me in the first 5 minutes of the visit. I really a simplistic yet professional landing.
