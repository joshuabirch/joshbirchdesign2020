About Me.

– Student, Designer, Researcher

Hello! I'm Josh, a UX designer from Birmingham, England. I currently study Computing Science at Staffordshire University and have developed a serious passion for UX design throughout my degree. While I have predominantly designed for web based products, I have serious aspirations to design in video games which resonates through my disseration which is titled "The Research and Development of Functional Immersive UI in Video Games". My design process is a little unorthodox as I am still new to the industry, however I am a huge fan of aesthetic design while also taking massive pride in responsive design.
